# Work Log Tracker Market Study
## Status
Research version 1.0, created/reviewed 2026-07-16; next review 2027-01-16.
## Findings
Individuals, freelancers, consultants, developers, students, and small-business owners need a lightweight record of completed work without surveillance or project-management overhead. [Toggl Track](https://toggl.com/track/), [Clockify](https://clockify.me/), and [Harvest](https://www.getharvest.com/) demonstrate demand for project grouping, timed/manual entries, billable classification, and summaries. Users value fast capture and clear reports; common friction includes timer complexity, paywalls, invasive monitoring, and billing coupling.
## Opportunity and boundaries
Ansiversa can offer private project-based work logs, accomplishments, blockers, combined filters, and accurate time summaries. Avoid screenshots, keystrokes, GPS, payroll, invoices, labor compliance, external integrations, and AI-generated claims. Categories and tags remain future research questions, not commitments.
