# Vaccination Tracker Marketing

## Product Promise

A private place to organize vaccination profiles, dose records, due dates, provider details, and vaccination history.

## Target Audiences

Parents, adults managing travel or annual vaccination records, and families who want a searchable personal record before speaking with healthcare providers.

## Feature Highlights

Profiles, vaccine labels, completed and upcoming records, multi-dose progress, due and overdue summaries, provider/manufacturer/batch/reference fields, and responsible insights.

## Responsible Medical Wording

Users should follow qualified healthcare professionals and official vaccination authorities. The app does not recommend vaccines, guarantee protection, replace official records, or create legal certificates.

## Short Promotional Concept

Keep vaccination history organized and easy to review, without turning personal records into medical advice.

## Video Direction

Show a calm family record workflow: create a profile, add a completed dose, enter provider and batch details, check an upcoming dose, and review insights. Avoid fear-based claims, medical diagnosis, immunity guarantees, or official certificate visuals.
