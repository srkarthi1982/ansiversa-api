# Vehicle Maintenance Tracker Marketing

## Status

Approved live marketing notes for version `1.0.0`.

## Positioning

Vehicle Maintenance Tracker is a protected Ansiversa workspace for vehicle profiles, maintenance history, reminders, upkeep costs, and odometer-based recordkeeping.

## Primary Route

`/vehicle-maintenance-tracker/vehicles`

## Audience

- Personal vehicle owners.
- Families managing multiple vehicles.
- Frequent drivers watching service intervals.
- Users who want maintenance memory without a repair marketplace.

## Core Value

Keep vehicle upkeep records, reminders, costs, and service history organized in one private workspace.

## Scope Boundary

Vehicle Maintenance Tracker does not provide diagnostics, mechanic booking, repair estimates, insurance processing, registration submission, telematics, legal compliance guarantees, or payment workflows.

## Search Keywords

Use vehicle maintenance tracker, car service log, maintenance reminder, oil change tracker, tire rotation reminder, inspection reminder, vehicle upkeep planner, and car maintenance records.

## Media Prompt

Generate a premium realistic productivity visual for Ansiversa Vehicle Maintenance Tracker. Show a calm dashboard with vehicle profiles, maintenance records, reminder cards, odometer values, and cost insights. Do not show mechanic marketplaces, live diagnostics, payment screens, insurance claims, or official government renewal workflows.
