## App Summary

Study Planner is an Ansiversa learning app that helps authenticated users create study plans, manage actionable tasks, record dated study logs, and review progress metrics.

The current live V1 supports owner-scoped plans, plan tasks, historical study logs, dashboard summaries, and review aggregates. Logs are historical records and are created rather than edited in the current API.

It does not schedule calendar events, send reminders, rebalance workload, sync with external learning systems, analyze subject performance deeply, or link to Course Tracker or Quiz performance in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Study Planner belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel focused, supportive, organized, student-friendly, calm, and progress-aware.

Avoid pressure-based study language, guarantee-grade claims, automated schedule claims, reminder claims before implementation, and exam-result promises.

## Core Value Proposition

Study Planner gives users a persistent planning and review workflow for study effort.

The value is structure and reflection, not automated scheduling or guaranteed academic performance.

## Problems We Solve

Study effort often gets split across plans, task lists, and memory of what was done.

Study Planner helps by connecting plans, tasks, logs, minutes, reflections, and review metrics in one owner-scoped workflow.
