## App Summary

Mood Journal is an Ansiversa wellness app that helps users record mood entries, notes, tags, dates, streaks, search, filtering, and local history in a browser-first journal workflow.

The current live V1 intentionally keeps private mood entries and journal notes out of backend systems. Runtime mood entry creation, history review, search, filtering, streak calculation, and deletion happen in the frontend after users open `/mood-journal/journal`.

It does not provide encrypted sync, account-backed history, exports, reminders, analytics, clinical workflows, or crisis-support integrations in the current version.
