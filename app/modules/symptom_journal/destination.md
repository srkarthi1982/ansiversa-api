# Symptom Journal Destination

## Destination Status

Unapproved for live launch. The app remains `active` / `comingSoon` / `version = null`.

## Product Destination

Symptom Journal should become a private personal health observation notebook where users can record symptoms, severity, body locations, triggers, relief methods tried, notes, and follow-up notes over time.

## Principles

- Personal record keeping only.
- Plain language and calm presentation.
- Owner-scoped data access.
- Summaries derived only from saved entries.
- No diagnosis, treatment, medication, emergency, or accuracy claims.

## V1 Scope

- Default and custom symptom categories.
- Symptom entry CRUD.
- Archive, restore, and delete.
- Search, filters, sorting, and pagination.
- Dashboard and insights summaries.
- Production-configured isolated database.

## Non Goals

- Diagnosing illnesses.
- Predicting diseases.
- Recommending treatment or medication.
- Emergency guidance.
- Healthcare provider or pharmacy integrations.
- AI symptom analysis.

## Journey Progress

V1 is prepared for manual verification and must remain behind the coming-soon gate until explicit approval.
