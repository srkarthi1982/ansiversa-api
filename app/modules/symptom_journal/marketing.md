# Symptom Journal Marketing

## Positioning

Symptom Journal is a private personal record-keeping tool for health observations. It helps users remember what happened, when it happened, how severe it felt, and what context they recorded.

## Guardrails

Do not claim that the app diagnoses, predicts disease, recommends treatment, recommends medication, replaces healthcare professionals, provides emergency guidance, or is medically accurate.

## Landing Copy

Record symptoms, severity, body areas, triggers, relief methods tried, and follow-up notes in one private journal.

## Video Prompt

Show a calm personal workflow: open the journal, create a dated symptom entry, choose a category, set severity, add body location and notes, review insights, and prepare to discuss observations with a qualified healthcare professional. Avoid alarming visuals, diagnosis language, medication advice, emergency scenes, or clinical certainty.

## Social Copy

Keep symptom observations organized over time with a private journal built for personal record keeping.

## SEO Notes

Focus on personal symptom journal, symptom tracker, health observation log, symptom history, and appointment preparation. Avoid diagnosis and treatment keywords as promises.
