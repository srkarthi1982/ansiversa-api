## Purpose

Rent a Car should become a trusted travel-planning workspace for comparing rental car options and keeping confirmed rental details accessible. It must remain a planning and recordkeeping tool, not a marketplace, booking engine, live pricing system, fleet manager, payment flow, or insurance advisor.

## Mature Product Identity

The mature product helps users answer:

- Which rental search am I planning?
- Which vehicle options did I compare?
- What is the estimated total for each option?
- Which option is preferred?
- What booking reference, provider contact, pickup instruction, and cancellation deadline did I confirm elsewhere?
- What patterns are visible across my rental planning history?

## Approved Workflow Direction

Searches -> Vehicles -> Bookings -> Insights

## V1 Destination Alignment

The Workflow Ready version implements the core direction with protected routes, owner-scoped records, local CRUD, duplicate actions, preferred option marking, calculated estimated totals, confirmed booking records, and deterministic insights.

## Future Enhancements

- More comparison-oriented views for one selected rental search.
- Optional reminder integration through approved platform notifications.
- More structured vehicle class and pickup method presets.
- Exportable trip summary if approved.

## Boundaries

Rent a Car must not claim direct booking, live inventory, live pricing, insurance advice, document storage for licenses/passports/payment cards, payment processing, SMS, email, chat, or external calendar integration unless separately approved.
