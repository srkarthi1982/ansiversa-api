# Rent a Car Marketing

## Framework

Ansiversa Marketing Framework v1.0

## Status

Coming soon. Not live.

## Positioning

Rent a Car is a private rental planning workspace for comparing user-entered vehicle options and recording confirmed booking details.

## Promise

Keep rental searches, vehicle comparisons, provider references, estimated totals, booking records, and cancellation deadlines organized in one Ansiversa workspace.

## Do Not Claim

- Direct booking
- Live rental inventory
- Live pricing
- Provider partnerships
- Discounts
- Insurance advice
- Payment processing
- License, passport, or payment-card document storage

## Primary Message

Plan rental searches, compare options, and keep confirmed booking details easy to revisit.

## Supporting Messages

- Save pickup and dropoff details, rental dates, budget, and notes.
- Compare providers, vehicle classes, estimated totals, deposits, and policies.
- Record confirmed booking references, provider contacts, pickup instructions, and cancellation deadlines.
- Review deterministic rental planning insights.
