# Savings Goal Planner Market Study

## Problem

People commonly save toward one-off targets but lose clarity when balances, deposits, withdrawals, deadlines, and interim milestones live in separate notes. Banking and investment products often optimize transfers or returns rather than transparent personal planning.

## Opportunity

Ansiversa can provide a private record for fixed-currency savings targets: owner-scoped goals, contribution history, reversible corrections, milestones, deterministic pace calculations, and per-currency summaries. This complements Expense Tracker and Bill Splitter without duplicating spending records, payments, banking, investments, or general life-goal management.

## Guardrails

The app records user-entered amounts only. It does not link accounts, move money, convert currency, predict returns, recommend investments, assess credit, lend, or provide financial or tax advice.
