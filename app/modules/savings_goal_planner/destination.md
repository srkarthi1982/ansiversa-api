## Product Boundary

Savings Goal Planner owns financial savings targets, starting balances, contributions, withdrawals, corrections, milestones, progress, and transparent deadline pace. Overfunding is prevented. Fully funded active goals complete automatically. Paused, cancelled, and archived goals reject transactions; archived goals are read-only until edited back to another state.

It does not own expenses, household bills, bank accounts, payments, investments, exchange rates, automated transfers, credit, lending, tax advice, AI recommendations, or broad non-financial life goals.

## Level 3 Destination

Protected goal history and details, three isolated tables, decimal-authoritative calculations, nested transaction and milestone management, combined filters and pagination, per-currency dashboard totals, direct URL safety, and responsive shared Ansiversa UI.
