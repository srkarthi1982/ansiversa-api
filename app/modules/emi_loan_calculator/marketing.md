# EMI / Loan Calculator Marketing

## Framework Version

Marketing Framework v1.0

## Product Positioning

EMI / Loan Calculator is a private planning workspace for estimating repayments, saving scenarios, comparing outcomes, and reviewing amortization schedules.

## One-Line Description

Estimate loan repayments, compare scenarios, and review amortization schedules in one private Ansiversa workspace.

## Core Message

Understand the repayment shape before you commit. EMI / Loan Calculator shows estimated EMI, interest, total repayment, payoff timing, and schedule details without acting as a lender or financial adviser.

## Current Features To Communicate

- Reducing-balance EMI calculation.
- Zero-interest loan handling.
- Optional start date, processing fee, and extra monthly payment.
- Full amortization schedule.
- Saved loan scenario CRUD.
- Scenario comparison.
- Saved-scenario insights.
- Currency display context for AED, USD, EUR, GBP, INR, and SAR.
- Planning-only disclaimer.

## Future Opportunities

- Exportable schedules.
- Print-friendly reports.
- Comparison charts.
- Annual schedule grouping.
- Governed AI explanations after financial-advice safeguards are approved.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise loan approval.
- Do not advertise live lender rates.
- Do not imply financial advice.
- Do not present Ansiversa as a bank, lender, broker, or marketplace.
- Do not claim currency conversion.
- Do not claim exact repayment amounts; use estimate language.

## Landing Page Copy

Estimate EMI, total interest, and payoff timing with a clear amortization schedule. Save scenarios privately, compare options, and review the numbers before discussing actual terms with a lender.

## SEO Notes

Target practical planning queries such as EMI calculator, loan payment calculator, loan amortization schedule, extra payment loan calculator, and loan repayment comparison. Keep page copy clear that results are estimates.

## Social Copy

Estimate monthly repayment, total interest, and payoff timing without lender noise. EMI / Loan Calculator keeps saved scenarios private inside Ansiversa.

## Video Prompt

Show a user entering loan amount, rate, duration, and extra monthly payment inside a clean Ansiversa workspace. The screen reveals EMI, interest, repayment total, and an amortization table, then compares two saved scenarios. Tone: calm, practical, private, planning-only.

## Current Implementation Alignment

Marketing must describe the current deterministic calculator, saved scenarios, comparison, and insights only. It must not advertise future exports, AI, lender data, or approval support as current behavior.
