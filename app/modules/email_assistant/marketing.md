## App Summary

Email Assistant is an Ansiversa productivity app for planning, organizing, and preparing professional email communication.

The current version gives users a structured workspace for email projects, drafts, reusable templates, and writing history. It helps users keep communication organized before anything is sent.

It does not send emails, connect to inboxes, import contacts, automate campaigns, track opens, or generate AI-written drafts in the current version.

Email Assistant should be marketed as a calm writing and organization workspace, not as an email automation platform.

## Core Value Proposition

Email Assistant gives users a focused place to prepare email communication without turning the product into a sending platform or campaign engine.

The value is not automation for its own sake. The value is clarity before communication.

Users can create projects, manage drafts, reuse templates, and review their writing history from one organized workspace.

## Problems We Solve

People often write important emails across scattered notes, copied templates, browser tabs, and forgotten drafts.

Email Assistant helps reduce that friction by giving each email effort a structured place to live.

It helps users:

- Separate email work by project
- Keep drafts organized
- Reuse templates without searching old messages
- Review recent writing activity
- Prepare communication more deliberately
- Avoid losing context across multiple email tasks

## Marketing Guardrails

Always say:

- Email Assistant helps users prepare and organize email communication.
- The current version supports projects, drafts, templates, and writing history.
- Users remain responsible for reviewing and sending their messages.
- The app is a preparation workspace, not a sending platform.

Never say:

- Email Assistant sends emails.
- Email Assistant connects to your inbox.
- Email Assistant imports your contacts.
- Email Assistant automates campaigns.
- Email Assistant tracks opens or clicks.
- Email Assistant writes perfect emails automatically.
- Email Assistant guarantees replies, conversions, jobs, sales, or approvals.
- Email Assistant replaces human judgment.

Legal and trust boundaries:

- Do not imply access to private inbox data.
- Do not imply delivery infrastructure.
- Do not imply professional legal, HR, or sales advice.
- Do not create fake email analytics in visuals.
- Do not show messages being sent from inside the product unless implemented.
