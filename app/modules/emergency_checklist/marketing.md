# Emergency Checklist Marketing

## Positioning

Emergency Checklist is a private preparedness planner for reusable emergency checklists inside Ansiversa.

## Short Description

Prepare reusable fire, medical, travel, home, vehicle, weather, and personal emergency checklists before they are needed.

## Long Description

Emergency Checklist helps users organize private preparedness lists by category, track completed and remaining items, reset checklists for repeat reviews, and archive stale plans. It is planning software, not emergency-response software.

## Audience

- Individuals
- Families
- Caregivers
- Travelers
- Homeowners
- Workers
