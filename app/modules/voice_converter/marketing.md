## App Summary

Voice Converter is an Ansiversa browser-first audio utility that provides a privacy-first text-to-speech workflow through browser-native Speech Synthesis.

The current live V1 lets users enter text, choose an available browser voice, preview speech locally, and save local sessions for History and Insights. The backend only serves catalog and overview metadata.

It does not store text or audio on the backend, export audio, sync sessions, auto-read text, integrate premium voice models, use cloud TTS providers, generate audio files, or use AI SDKs in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Voice Converter belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel private, accessible, lightweight, practical, calm, and browser-native.

Avoid premium voice claims, AI voice claims, export claims, cloud TTS claims, voice cloning language, and backend audio-processing implications.

## Core Value Proposition

Voice Converter gives users a simple browser-native way to hear text spoken aloud while keeping text, audio, and sessions out of backend runtime systems.

The value is local preview and accessibility, not premium voice generation or audio export.

## Problems We Solve

Voice Converter helps by using browser Speech Synthesis, available local voices, and local session records.
