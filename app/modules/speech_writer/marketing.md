## App Summary

Speech Writer is an Ansiversa writing app that helps authenticated users plan, draft, edit, and review speeches through event-oriented projects, speech drafts, reusable templates, and history.

The current live V1 stores user-authored speech projects, speeches, templates, and history. It supports manual drafting and review workflows.

It does not generate speech text with AI, export speeches, automate revision history, provide delivery rehearsal scoring, or generate outlines automatically in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Speech Writer belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel thoughtful, human, structured, event-aware, calm, and respectful of the speaker's voice.

Avoid AI ghostwriting claims, guaranteed applause language, public speaking cure claims, export claims before implementation, and performance scoring claims.

Speech writing often begins in scattered notes, copied outlines, and unfinished drafts.

Speech Writer helps by connecting event context, audience, tone, purpose, drafts, templates, and review history.
