## App Summary

Interview Coach is an Ansiversa career app that helps authenticated users structure written interview preparation around sessions, questions, practice answers, and readiness reviews.

The current live V1 stores interview sessions, role and company context, interview type, target date, questions, written answers, confidence ratings, answer status, and manual review notes with strengths, improvements, next steps, and readiness scores.

It does not include video practice, audio capture, speech scoring, automated interviewer simulation, recruiter collaboration, calendar scheduling, AI-generated questions, or hiring prediction in the current version.

## Problems We Solve

Interview preparation often gets scattered across notes, job descriptions, old documents, and memory.

Interview Coach helps by:

- Grouping preparation by interview session
- Storing questions under each session
- Keeping written answers connected to questions
- Recording confidence ratings
- Preserving manual review notes
- Summarizing preparation state through dashboard counters
- Helping users return to active preparation quickly
