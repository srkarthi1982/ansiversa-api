# Travel Itinerary Builder Destination

## Destination

Travel Itinerary Builder should become a practical trip-planning workspace for everyday users who need a lightweight place to keep itineraries, days, activities, locations, and booking references organized without adopting a full travel-management suite.

## Mature Product Shape

The mature product should help users:

- Plan one trip at a time or maintain several upcoming trips.
- Break each trip into itinerary days.
- Add timed or untimed activities.
- Group activities by category.
- Keep notes and booking references close to the schedule.
- Review trip readiness and planning coverage.

## Current Journey Progress

`20 / 100`

The current implementation provides the core private itinerary, day, activity, category, and insight workflows. It does not yet include integrations such as maps, reservation imports, collaboration, attachments, exports, or AI planning.

## Destination Status

`draft`

## Current Boundaries

The app does not own flights, hotel booking, payments, live travel advisories, map routing, production user communication, or external reservation ingestion. Those features require separate Partner/Astra approval.
