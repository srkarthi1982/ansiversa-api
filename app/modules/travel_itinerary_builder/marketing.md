# Travel Itinerary Builder Marketing

## Product Positioning

Travel Itinerary Builder is a private planning workspace for turning a trip idea into a dated itinerary with days, activities, locations, categories, and booking references.

## Primary Audience

- Families planning vacations.
- Solo travelers organizing personal trips.
- Business travelers who need lightweight schedule notes.
- Local weekend travelers planning short breaks.

## Value Proposition

Keep trip dates, daily plans, activities, notes, and references in one protected Ansiversa workflow.

## Core Messages

- Create a trip plan quickly.
- Organize each travel day.
- Add activities with times, locations, and booking references.
- Use categories to scan the itinerary faster.
- Review trip coverage through simple insights.

## Current Scope

The current product is a private itinerary planner. It is not a travel marketplace, booking engine, map-routing tool, group collaboration product, or AI travel agent.
