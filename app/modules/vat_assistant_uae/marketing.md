# VAT Assistant UAE Marketing

## Framework Version

Marketing Framework v1.0

## Product Positioning

VAT Assistant UAE is a private VAT recordkeeping workspace for UAE businesses that want to organize registrations, returns, transactions, and estimate-only summaries before professional review or official filing.

## Current Feature Messaging

- Organize VAT registration details and TRN notes.
- Track VAT returns with output VAT, input VAT, payable VAT, refund amount, and filing status.
- Record transaction-level VAT amounts, rates, invoices, counterparties, and notes.
- Review VAT summaries by period and rate.
- Keep official filing and advice boundaries visible.

## Public Landing Copy

VAT Assistant UAE helps you keep VAT preparation records in one private workspace. Create registration records, track return periods, record transaction VAT amounts, and review summaries before external filing or adviser review.

## Guardrails

- Do not advertise official FTA filing or EmaraTax submission.
- Do not claim tax advice, compliance certification, or accountant replacement.
- Do not imply UAEPass, FTA, EmaraTax, or government integration.
- Do not state that estimates are complete, official, or filing-ready.
- Future exports, adviser collaboration, imports, and AI review must be described only as future possibilities unless implemented and approved.

## Current Implementation Alignment

Marketing must reflect only the Workflow Ready V1 implementation: protected CRUD for registrations, returns, and transactions; deterministic insights; owner-scoped storage; overview metadata; and visible disclaimers.
