# Water Intake Tracker Marketing

## Positioning

Water Intake Tracker is a private hydration logging workspace for people who want to record daily water intake and review personal trends.

## Core Message

Log water entries, set your own daily goal, and review hydration history without turning a simple wellness habit into medical advice.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim the app diagnoses dehydration.
- Do not claim it prescribes how much water a person should drink.
- Do not claim health outcomes.
- Do not imply professional healthcare replacement.
- Always describe goals as user-managed and personal.

## Landing Copy

Track water intake with simple entries, flexible units, drink type labels, and daily progress against a goal you control.

## Short Description

A private personal tracker for water entries, daily goals, and hydration summaries.

## Video Prompt Direction

Show a calm mobile and desktop workflow where a user adds a water entry, checks today's progress, reviews weekly summaries, and edits a goal. Avoid medical imagery, doctors, diagnosis screens, or health-outcome claims.
