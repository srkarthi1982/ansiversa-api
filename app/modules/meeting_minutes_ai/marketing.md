## App Summary

Meeting Minutes AI is an Ansiversa productivity app that helps authenticated users structure meeting context, notes, action items, and summaries.

The current live V1 stores meeting records, manually entered notes or transcripts, action items, and summaries with decisions and risks. Dashboard responses use previews and counters so long content stays out of list payloads.

It does not include live transcription, audio ingestion, speaker diarization, calendar import, external task sync, or automated AI summary generation from recordings in the current version.

## Core Value Proposition

The app gives meeting follow-up a structured, owner-scoped workspace.

The current value is organization and review, not automated transcription or AI-generated minutes.

## Problems We Solve

Meeting follow-up often disappears into long notes, chat messages, and unclear ownership.

Meeting Minutes AI helps by:

- Storing meeting context
- Keeping notes or transcripts under the meeting
- Tracking action items with owners and due dates
- Recording summaries, decisions, and risks
- Showing dashboard counters and previews
- Separating long content from lightweight lists
