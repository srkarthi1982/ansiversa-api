# Birthday & Anniversary Reminder Marketing

## Positioning

Birthday & Anniversary Reminder helps users remember meaningful annual occasions and prepare in advance.

## Audience

- Families
- Friends
- Professionals
- Anyone who tracks birthdays, anniversaries, graduations, and milestones

## Core Messages

- Keep important dates in one protected workspace.
- See what is today, this week, this month, and next 30 days.
- Save gift ideas and notes.
- Acknowledge this year's occasion without losing next year's reminder.

## Allowed Claims

- Reminder CRUD.
- Occasion types.
- Favourite, archive, restore, and acknowledge actions.
- Annual next occurrence and days remaining.
- Dashboard and insights from stored records.

## Not Allowed

- Automatic SMS, WhatsApp, email, or notification delivery.
- Contact scraping.
- AI-generated gifts or messages in V1.

## Short Copy

Remember the date. Prepare the thought. Mark it handled.

## Video Prompt

Show a user adding a birthday, saving gift ideas, checking next 30 days, marking a reminder favourite, and acknowledging this year's occasion. End with a clear note that messages are not sent automatically.
