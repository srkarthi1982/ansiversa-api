# Salary Breakdown Calculator Destination

## Status

Destination approval is pending. No reviewed date is recorded.

## Boundary

The app owns private hypothetical salary scenarios, earnings, deductions, normalized recurring/one-time breakdowns, and factual two-scenario comparison. It does not own employees, payroll runs, official payslips, statutory rules, tax filing, employer reports, transfers, exchange rates, market benchmarking, AI recommendations, or legal/financial advice.

Level 3 includes an isolated three-table database, protected owner-scoped APIs, fixed-precision calculations, combined list filters and pagination, per-currency dashboard summaries, and cross-currency comparison safeguards.
