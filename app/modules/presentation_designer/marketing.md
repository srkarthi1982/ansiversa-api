## App Summary

Presentation Designer is an Ansiversa productivity app that helps authenticated users organize presentation projects, ordered slides, reusable assets, and review activity.

The current live V1 stores planning data, slide text, asset references, and review history. It supports projects, slides, assets, and review records with owner-scoped editing and lightweight dashboard summaries.

It does not render slide files, export PowerPoint or PDF artifacts, store binary uploads, generate slide previews, or provide full approval workflows in the current version.

## Problems We Solve

Presentation work often spreads across notes, slide drafts, assets, and feedback threads.

Presentation Designer helps by:

- Creating presentation projects
- Ordering slides
- Storing reusable asset references
- Logging review, export, or presentation activity
- Separating list summaries from full editable records
- Keeping owner-scoped deck records together
