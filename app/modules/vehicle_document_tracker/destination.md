# Vehicle Document Tracker Destination

## Product Boundary

Vehicle Document Tracker is a personal document tracking and reminder tool for vehicle-related records. It helps users organize metadata about registrations, insurance, inspections, warranties, service contracts, and custom document types.

It does not replace official government registration systems, renew documents automatically, provide legal advice, verify legal compliance, or act as an insurance provider portal.

## V1 Destination

- Create, edit, archive, restore, and delete vehicles.
- Create, edit, archive, restore, and delete vehicle document records.
- Manage custom document types alongside safe default types.
- Track issue dates, expiry dates, renewal reminder dates, issuing authorities, statuses, and notes.
- Search, filter, sort, and review stored records.
- Summarize expiring, expired, upcoming, recently updated, by-type, and by-vehicle records from stored data only.

## Non-Goals

- No document file uploads in V1.
- No OCR or document scanning.
- No automatic renewal workflows.
- No government, insurance, or inspection-provider integrations.
- No legal compliance certification.
