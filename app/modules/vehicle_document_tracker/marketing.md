# Vehicle Document Tracker Marketing

## Positioning

Vehicle Document Tracker is a personal vehicle paperwork organizer for people who want a clearer view of important document dates and renewal reminders.

## Core Message

Keep vehicle registrations, insurance, inspections, warranties, and renewal dates organized in one calm personal tracker.

## Guardrails

Do not claim the app renews documents, replaces official government systems, verifies legal compliance, provides legal advice, acts as an insurance provider portal, stores official documents, scans documents, or validates document authenticity.

## Demo Story

Show a user adding a vehicle, creating a document record for insurance, setting an expiry date and reminder date, filtering upcoming renewals, and reviewing insights by vehicle and document type. Avoid official seals, government dashboards, insurance claim portals, legal advice language, and file-upload/OCR visuals.

## Landing Copy

Vehicle Document Tracker helps you organize vehicle paperwork references and renewal dates. Track registrations, insurance, inspection certificates, warranties, service contracts, and custom document types from your own saved records.

## SEO Notes

Focus on personal vehicle document tracker, car document organizer, vehicle registration reminder, insurance expiry tracker, inspection reminder, warranty tracker, and vehicle paperwork log. Avoid government renewal, legal compliance, insurance provider, official registration, and document verification claims.
