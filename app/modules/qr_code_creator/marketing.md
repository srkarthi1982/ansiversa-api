## App Summary

QR Code Creator is an Ansiversa browser-first utility app for generating QR codes locally.

The current live V1 keeps QR content, preview generation, copy, and download actions in the frontend at `/qr-code-creator/generator`. The backend only serves catalog and overview metadata.

It does not store QR projects, generated images, scan events, campaign records, account-backed QR history, public redirect links, scan analytics, or branded templates in the current version.

## Brand Identity

QR Code Creator belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel practical, private, fast, clean, utility-focused, and transparent.

Avoid campaign analytics claims, scan tracking claims, public redirect claims, backend image storage claims, and marketing automation visuals.
