# Bill Splitter Market Study
## Status
Research version 1.0, created/reviewed 2026-07-16; next review 2027-01-16.
## Findings
Restaurant groups, travelers, households, and teams need a transparent way to assign bill items and track balances. [Splitwise](https://www.splitwise.com/), [Tab](https://www.tabapp.co/), and [Tricount](https://tricount.com/) demonstrate demand for group participants, itemized splits, remainder handling, and settlement visibility. Users value clarity and fast mobile entry; common friction includes forced accounts, opaque rounding, ads, payment coupling, and broader recurring-ledger complexity.
## Ansiversa opportunity
Provide a focused one-bill workflow with participants, items, equal/single/custom allocations, fees, recorded paid amounts, and exact server reconciliation. Avoid banking, payment processing, card storage, OCR, currency conversion, accounting integrations, financial advice, and tax claims.
