# Trip Cost Calculator Marketing

## Document Status

**Status:** Workflow Ready Draft

**Marketing Framework Version:** 1.0

**App:** Trip Cost Calculator

**Purpose:** Single source of truth for future marketing, campaign generation, landing page copy, advertisements, and video prompts.

## App Summary

Trip Cost Calculator is an Ansiversa Mobility & Travel app that helps authenticated users create trips, add category cost items, compare total costs, and review spending insights.

The current V1 product is Workflow Ready only and remains `comingSoon`. It stores owner-scoped trip and cost records in an isolated backend database and does not provide live pricing, bookings, payments, reimbursements, route maps, or accounting automation.

## Brand Identity

Trip Cost Calculator should feel practical, clear, grounded, and travel-aware. Marketing should emphasize private cost clarity, not promises of official travel prices.

## Elevator Pitch

Trip Cost Calculator gives users a focused place to record trip costs, compare totals, and understand cost per traveler, cost per kilometer, categories, and monthly activity.

## Tagline Options

1. Know what each trip costs.
2. Plan the trip. Track the cost.
3. Travel spending, organized.
4. Compare trips with clarity.
5. From fuel to food, see the total.

## Core Value Proposition

Trips include many small costs that are easy to forget. Trip Cost Calculator keeps trip context and cost items together so users can understand the total and compare trips over time.

## Target Audience

- Road-trip planners.
- Families and groups reviewing costs.
- Frequent travelers.
- Drivers comparing routes.
- Users preparing budget or reimbursement notes.

## Key Features To Highlight

- Trip CRUD and duplicate.
- Cost item CRUD by category.
- Cost per traveler.
- Cost per kilometer.
- Category breakdowns.
- Monthly spending and recent activity.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim live booking, live routing, live fuel prices, payment splitting, reimbursement approval, tax filing, or accounting sync.
- Do not suggest costs are official or complete.
- Do not imply currency conversion exists in V1.

Explore Trip Cost Calculator at `/trip-cost-calculator/trips`.
