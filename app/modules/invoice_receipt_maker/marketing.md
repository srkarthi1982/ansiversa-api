## App Summary

Invoice and Receipt Maker is an Ansiversa business app that helps authenticated users organize billing projects, create invoice or receipt records, add line items, calculate document totals, and log document activity.

The current live V1 stores structured billing data and recalculates line-item totals. It does not generate PDFs, send emails, collect online payments, manage tax jurisdiction rules, or provide accounting advice.

## Problems We Solve

Small billing workflows often get scattered across spreadsheets, notes, and old files.

Invoice and Receipt Maker helps by:

- Grouping documents under billing projects
- Capturing business and client context
- Storing invoice and receipt records
- Managing itemized charges
- Recalculating totals when items change
- Logging activity history
- Keeping records owner-scoped
