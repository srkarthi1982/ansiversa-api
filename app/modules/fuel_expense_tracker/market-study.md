# Fuel Expense Tracker Market Study

## User Need

Drivers often remember the most recent fuel purchase but lose the long-term picture: monthly spend, station habits, vehicle-level cost, and fuel economy trends. A lightweight tracker solves this without requiring banking connections or vehicle integrations.

## Core Jobs

- Record a fuel purchase quickly from a receipt.
- Keep purchases tied to the right vehicle.
- Review monthly fuel spending.
- Estimate fuel economy from odometer readings.
- Compare station and vehicle spending.

## Differentiation

Fuel Expense Tracker is intentionally narrower than general expense tools and vehicle maintenance tools. It focuses on fuel records only, which keeps the workflow quick and reduces ambiguity.

## Trust Boundaries

The app should never imply that fuel economy estimates diagnose a vehicle issue. It should not recommend repairs or promise savings. It should present summaries as user-entered records and estimates only.
