# Fuel Expense Tracker Marketing

## Positioning

Fuel Expense Tracker is a personal vehicle fuel log for recording purchases, fuel quantity, odometer readings, station details, payment notes, and spending summaries.

## Guardrails

Do not claim the app diagnoses vehicle problems, recommends repairs, connects to vehicle systems, replaces mechanic advice, or guarantees fuel savings.

## Landing Copy

Track fuel purchases across your vehicles, review monthly fuel costs, compare station spending, and estimate fuel economy from your own odometer entries.

## Video Prompt

Show a user adding a car, logging a fuel receipt with odometer and station details, reviewing total monthly cost, comparing vehicle spend, and checking recent entries. Avoid dashboard-warning visuals, mechanic advice, repair recommendations, connected-car claims, or guaranteed savings language.

## Social Copy

Keep fuel receipts organized by vehicle and understand monthly fuel spending from your own records.

## SEO Notes

Focus on fuel expense tracker, fuel logbook, vehicle fuel cost tracker, fuel purchase history, and gas expense tracker. Avoid diagnostic, repair, and fuel-saving guarantee language.
