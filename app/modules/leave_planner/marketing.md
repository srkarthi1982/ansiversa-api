# Leave Planner Marketing Framework

## Positioning

Plan personal leave and understand your balance without a full HR system.

## Supported claims

- Define personal leave types and allowances.
- Record full or half weekday leave.
- Track user-managed planned, pending, approved, taken, cancelled, and rejected states.
- Review used, planned, and remaining balances.
- Search and filter upcoming and historical leave.

## Claims to avoid

No employer approval, payroll, attendance, legal entitlement, government guidance, notifications, calendar sync, or AI. Status values are personal organizational labels.
