# Leave Planner Market Study

## Identity

App #090, Leave Planner (`leave-planner`), Work & Planning, review-stage only.

## Need and market boundary

Individuals often track annual allowances, planned absences, and consumed leave in spreadsheets or employer portals that are unavailable outside one workplace. Leave Planner provides a private personal record without becoming an HR system. Employer administration, payroll, attendance, approvals, legal entitlement calculations, notifications, external calendars, AI, and third-party integrations are excluded.

## V1 decision

Users define custom leave types and allowances, record full or half weekday leave, use personal planning statuses, prevent overlapping active entries, search and filter history, and review balances. Weekends are excluded; public holidays are not modeled. Negative remaining balances are allowed and visibly reported rather than blocked.
