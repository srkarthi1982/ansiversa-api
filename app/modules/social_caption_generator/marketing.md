## App Summary

Social Caption Generator is an Ansiversa marketing productivity app that helps authenticated users organize social caption projects, editable captions, reusable templates, and caption history.

The current live V1 stores campaign-style projects, caption records, templates, and history in an owner-scoped backend. It stores generated and edited caption content, but it does not call an AI generation provider directly.

It does not provide AI caption generation, scheduled publishing, social network integrations, analytics feedback, search, bulk publishing, or automated history in the current version.
