# Net Worth Tracker Marketing

## Positioning

Understand the asset and liability balances you record, without connecting financial accounts.

## Truthful capabilities

- Maintain private asset and liability accounts.
- Preserve balance history and dated snapshots.
- Review assets, liabilities, and net worth separately by currency.
- Compare captured snapshots without currency conversion or advice.

All values are user-entered planning records, not verified live valuations or official financial statements.
