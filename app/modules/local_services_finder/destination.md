# Local Services Finder Destination

## Product Identity

Local Services Finder is App #099 (`local-services-finder`) in the Daily Life category. It is a private personal directory for trusted local providers, not a public business search engine, marketplace, booking platform, map, or review product.

Destination Status: approved
Current Journey Progress: 20 / 100
Reviewed At: 2026-07-19

## Mature Destination

At 100 / 100, Local Services Finder helps users keep trusted local service providers organized with categories, contact details, personal notes, ratings, preferred status, archive history, and review surfaces. It should make known providers easy to retrieve without depending on external discovery engines.

## Core Workflow

1. Create service categories.
2. Add local service providers with contact and address information.
3. Mark trusted providers as preferred.
4. Rate providers from 1 to 5 based on personal experience.
5. Record last contacted dates and notes.
6. Archive stale records and restore when needed.

## Product Principles

- User-entered data is authoritative.
- Private notes remain owner-scoped.
- The app avoids public rankings, ads, and marketplace incentives.
- Archived providers are preserved but read-only until restored.
- The app stays inside the persistent Ansiversa shell.

## Non-Goals

- Google Maps replacement.
- Yelp replacement.
- Booking system.
- Marketplace.
- Review platform.
- GPS, route planning, or navigation.
- External directory search.
- Third-party APIs.
