# Local Services Finder Marketing

## Framework Version

Marketing Framework v1.0

## Product Positioning

Local Services Finder is a private directory for trusted local service providers inside Ansiversa. It helps users keep known providers, contact details, ratings, preferred markers, addresses, and notes organized without becoming a public search engine or marketplace.

## Truthful Current Capability

Current Workflow Ready scope includes:

- Protected providers workflow.
- Category management.
- Create, edit, delete, prefer, unprefer, archive, and restore.
- Search, filters, and pagination.
- Dashboard metrics for providers, preferred, archived, recently contacted, and categories.

## Public One-Liner

Keep trusted local service providers organized with contact details, categories, ratings, notes, and preferred markers.

## Landing Page Copy

### Hero

Local Services Finder

Build a private directory of providers you already know and trust.

### Value Props

- Save electricians, plumbers, doctors, pharmacies, tutors, salons, car repair shops, and more.
- Track phone, email, website, address, area, notes, rating, preferred state, and last contacted date.
- Archive stale providers without losing your own service memory.

## Video Prompt

Show a calm Ansiversa workspace where a user creates categories, adds a plumber and pharmacy provider, marks one preferred, filters by rating, opens provider details, and archives an old provider. Do not show maps, route planning, public reviews, booking, marketplace matching, or AI recommendations.

## Social Copy

Local Services Finder keeps your trusted local providers in one private directory: who to call, where they are, what you noted, and who you prefer.

## SEO Keywords

local services directory, trusted provider list, service provider organizer, private contact directory, local provider tracker, home service contacts

## Guardrails

Do not claim maps, GPS, directions, route optimization, third-party directory search, booking, quotes, marketplace matching, public reviews, business verification, emergency dispatch, or AI recommendations.

## Future Marketing Opportunities

If later approved and implemented, marketing may discuss reminders, imports, exports, household sharing, or contact history. These are not current V1 capabilities.
