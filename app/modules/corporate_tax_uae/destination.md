# Corporate Tax UAE Destination

## Product Identity

Corporate Tax UAE is a private UAE business record-keeping and estimate workspace. It helps users organize corporate-tax periods, user-entered adjustments, filing-related obligations, and transparent calculation assumptions before professional review or external filing.

It is not legal, accounting, audit, filing, or tax advice. It does not replace the UAE Federal Tax Authority, Ministry of Finance, EmaraTax, an auditor, accountant, or tax adviser.

## Destination Vision

The mature product should become a review-ready preparation workspace for UAE businesses: periods are structured, assumptions are visible, obligations are trackable, supporting documents can be attached, and adviser collaboration is possible without implying official compliance or filing acceptance.

## V1 Scope

- Owner-scoped tax period CRUD.
- User-entered adjustment CRUD.
- Obligation CRUD and local completion action.
- Estimate-only insights based on stored records.
- Explicit tax rate, threshold, effective-date, and reference assumptions per period.
- Currency-grouped summaries to avoid silent conversion.
- Documentation and visible product boundary.

## Future Possibilities

- Configurable tax-rule assumption library.
- Adviser collaboration and review comments.
- Document attachments for support schedules.
- Audit trail for changes to periods, assumptions, adjustments, and obligations.
- Exportable calculation schedules.
- Filing-preparation checklist.
- Multiple entities and entity groups.
- Currency normalization with explicit FX assumptions.
- Official-reference links and review reminders.
- Read-only adviser sharing.

## Non-Goals

- Official FTA integration.
- Filing returns from Ansiversa.
- Eligibility or exemption determinations.
- Tax advice, accounting advice, legal advice, or audit opinion.
- Guaranteed compliance claims.
