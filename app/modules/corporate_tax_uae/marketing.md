# Corporate Tax UAE Marketing

## Positioning

Corporate Tax UAE is a private preparation workspace for UAE businesses that need to organize tax periods, adjustment notes, obligations, and estimate assumptions before adviser review or external filing.

## Core Message

Keep UAE corporate-tax preparation records structured, transparent, and ready for professional review.

## Audience

- UAE business owners.
- Founders and operators.
- Finance/admin teams.
- Small teams preparing records for accountants or tax advisers.

## Value Props

- Organize tax periods by entity and financial year.
- Track user-entered taxable-income adjustments.
- Record obligations, deadlines, responsible people, and completion status.
- Review estimate-only summaries with visible assumptions.
- Avoid hidden currency mixing by grouping totals by currency.

## Product Boundary

Corporate Tax UAE stores user-entered planning records and produces estimates from configured assumptions. It does not provide legal, accounting, audit, filing, or tax advice. Users must verify current requirements and calculations with the UAE Federal Tax Authority and qualified professionals.
