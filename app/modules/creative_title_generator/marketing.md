## App Summary

Creative Title Generator is an Ansiversa content ideation app that helps authenticated users save title briefs, generate deterministic title options, review scored results, and revisit generation history. The current live V1 keeps projects, generated titles, and jobs owner-scoped, with lightweight list responses and detail views.

The product supports honest title brainstorming. It is not a full writing suite, SEO platform, ad optimization service, publishing tool, or unreviewed AI content factory.

## Brand Identity

Creative Title Generator belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem. Every marketing asset should feel simple, trustworthy, intelligent, professional, accessible, and globally understandable.

The brand identity should communicate:

- Simplicity: title ideation should be fast and organized.
- Trust: titles should fit the actual topic and audience.
- Intelligence: options should support comparison, not promise performance.
- Professionalism: useful for creators, marketers, students, founders, and freelancers.
- Accessibility: users should feel creatively unstuck without needing a full writing suite.
- Honest creativity: the emotional center is better options without clickbait.

Avoid:

- Traffic, ranking, virality, or click-through guarantees.
- Misleading urgency or unsupported claims.
- SEO automation positioning.
- AI provider-backed generation claims as current features.
- Copying headline formulas, scoring systems, or examples from competitors.

## Core Value Proposition

Title ideas often get scattered across chats, notes, drafts, and memory. Creative Title Generator keeps the brief, generated options, placeholder scores, and job history connected so users can return to the creative thinking later.

Its strongest value is focused ideation with context. V1 uses deterministic template-based generation, not real AI title generation, and it avoids traffic or SEO performance promises.

## Problems We Solve

- Users need many title options before one feels right.
- One-off title ideas are hard to compare later.
- Topic, audience, language, type, style, and keywords often get separated from generated results.
- Generic title tools can encourage formulaic or clickbait output.
- AI can invent benefits, numbers, claims, or urgency.
- Users need title help without entering a full content suite.
