# Errand Planner Destination

## Product Identity

Errand Planner is App #098 (`errand-planner`) in the Daily Life category. At maturity, it should be a calm personal errand workspace for organizing practical outside-the-home tasks without becoming a map, delivery tracker, calendar, or project-management replacement.

Destination Status: approved
Current Journey Progress: 20 / 100
Reviewed At: 2026-07-19

## Mature Destination

At 100 / 100, Errand Planner helps users decide what errands need attention, where they happen, when they are due, and what is already complete. It should make everyday errands easier to prepare and review while preserving privacy and user control.

## Core Workflow

1. Create categories for common errand types.
2. Add errands with title, notes, priority, due date, location, estimate, and status.
3. Review pending, in-progress, overdue, due-today, due-soon, completed, archived, and cancelled records.
4. Complete errands when handled.
5. Reopen, archive, restore, edit, or delete records as needed.

## Product Principles

- Fast capture before heavy planning.
- User-entered information stays authoritative.
- Errand status remains transparent and reversible.
- Archived records are preserved but read-only until restored.
- The product stays inside the persistent Ansiversa shell.

## Non-Goals

- GPS navigation.
- Route optimization.
- Delivery tracking.
- Calendar replacement.
- Task-management replacement.
- AI assistant or automation.
- Shared household collaboration in V1.

## Maturity Roadmap

- 20 / 100: Private CRUD, categories, priorities, due dates, locations, completion, archive, filters, pagination, dashboard metrics.
- 40 / 100: Better recurring errand and saved template support if approved.
- 60 / 100: Shared notification integration if the platform notification architecture is approved.
- 80 / 100: Household or small-team errand sharing if the product boundary is approved.
- 100 / 100: Reliable, private, cross-device errand planning with reminders, review surfaces, and clear boundaries.
