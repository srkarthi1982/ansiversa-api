# Errand Planner Marketing

## Framework Version

Marketing Framework v1.0

## Product Positioning

Errand Planner is a private everyday errand workspace inside Ansiversa. It helps users capture errands, organize them by category, assign priority and due dates, record locations, estimate duration, and track completion.

## Truthful Current Capability

Current Workflow Ready scope includes:

- Protected errands workflow.
- Category management.
- Create, edit, delete, complete, reopen, archive, and restore.
- Search, filters, and pagination.
- Dashboard metrics for pending, completed, overdue, due today, due soon, archived, and cancelled errands.

## Public One-Liner

Plan everyday errands with priorities, due dates, categories, locations, notes, and clear completion status.

## Landing Page Copy

### Hero

Errand Planner

Keep groceries, pharmacy trips, bank visits, courier tasks, school errands, and household runs organized in one private workspace.

### Value Props

- See what is pending, overdue, due today, and due soon.
- Organize errands by category, priority, due date, location, and notes.
- Complete, reopen, archive, restore, and review errands without a heavy task-management system.

## Video Prompt

Show a calm Ansiversa workspace where a user creates grocery, pharmacy, and courier errands, filters by due today, completes one errand, and archives a completed record. The mood should be practical, private, and organized. Do not show maps, delivery tracking, AI automation, or route optimization.

## Social Copy

Errand Planner helps you keep everyday trips and tasks visible: what to do, where to go, when it is due, and what is already done.

## SEO Keywords

errand planner, personal errand tracker, daily errands, grocery task planner, private task list, due date tracker, household errand organizer

## Guardrails

Do not claim GPS navigation, maps, route optimization, delivery tracking, reminders, push notifications, calendar sync, collaboration, or AI assistance. Do not imply legal, government, medical, financial, or logistics authority.

## Future Marketing Opportunities

If later approved and implemented, marketing may discuss reminders, recurring errands, household sharing, or platform notifications. These are not current V1 capabilities.
