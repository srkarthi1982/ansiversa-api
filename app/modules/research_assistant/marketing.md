## App Summary

Research Assistant is an Ansiversa productivity and learning app that helps authenticated users organize research topics, notes, references, and status.

The current live V1 stores manually entered research topics, notes, and references in an owner-scoped backend. Users can move topics through collecting, reviewing, and complete states.

It does not crawl sources, generate citations, run external research jobs, synthesize long-form reports, import sources, or create AI research summaries in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Research Assistant belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel thoughtful, organized, academic, careful, trustworthy, and human-led.

Avoid automated research claims, source crawling claims, citation-generation claims, AI synthesis claims before implementation, and academic shortcut language.

## Core Value Proposition

Research Assistant gives users a persistent place to structure manually entered research material.

The value is organization and review, not automated source discovery or AI synthesis in V1.

## Problems We Solve

Research work often gets scattered across bookmarks, notes, copied text, and unfinished outlines.

Research Assistant helps by creating topic records, saving notes, saving references, and moving topics through a simple research lifecycle.
