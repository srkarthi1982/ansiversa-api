## App Summary

Time Zone Scheduler is an Ansiversa browser-local productivity app for comparing meeting times across participant time zones.

The current live V1 lets users create local schedules, highlight a My Time zone, compare converted times through browser-native `Intl` APIs, save schedules in browser storage, and review local insights.

It does not store schedules on the backend, send calendar invitations, sync calendars, send notifications, support recurring meetings, run availability polls, or synchronize history across devices in the current version.
