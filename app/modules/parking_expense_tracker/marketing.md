# Parking Expense Tracker Marketing

## Status

Workflow Ready marketing notes. Not live.

## Positioning

Parking Expense Tracker is a protected Ansiversa workspace for parking expenses, reusable parking locations, vehicles, payment methods, and monthly spending visibility.

## Primary Route

`/parking-expense-tracker/expenses`

## Core Message

Track where you parked, what you paid, how long you stayed, which vehicle was involved, and how parking costs change over time.

## Audience

- Commuters
- Frequent drivers
- Families managing vehicle costs
- Users preparing personal parking records before reimbursement or budgeting review

## Scope Boundary

Parking Expense Tracker does not provide parking reservations, live availability, payments, receipt forwarding, reimbursement approval, tax filing, accounting sync, GPS tracking, or official compliance workflows.

## Visual Direction

Use calm mobility and expense visuals: parking receipts, simple location cards, monthly spending summaries, payment method breakdowns, and vehicle labels. Avoid garage marketplace, live map reservation, payment checkout, parking tickets, or enforcement imagery.

## Image Generation Prompt

Generate a premium realistic productivity visual for Ansiversa Parking Expense Tracker. Show a calm dashboard with parking expense cards, location summaries, vehicle labels, payment method breakdowns, and monthly parking spend. Do not show parking reservation maps, payment checkout, police enforcement, parking fines, or accounting software.
