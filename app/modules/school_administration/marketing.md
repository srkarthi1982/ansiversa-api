# School Administration Marketing

## Positioning

School Administration is a private record workspace for smaller schools, tutors, training centres, and early-stage institutions that need to organize students, classes, enrolments, attendance, and operational summaries without adopting a full SIS.

## Primary Message

Keep school records organized in one calm workspace.

## Value Propositions

- Manage private student records.
- Organize classes and capacity.
- Assign students through explicit enrolments.
- Record attendance with duplicate safeguards.
- Review summaries based only on stored data.

## Boundaries

School Administration V1 is not a complete SIS, LMS, payments platform, admissions system, parent/student portal, biometric attendance system, grading tool, or government reporting system.
