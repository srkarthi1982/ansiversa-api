# Driver Logbook Marketing

## Positioning

Driver Logbook is a personal driving journal for recording trips, vehicle use, odometer readings, trip purposes, locations, and distance summaries.

## Guardrails

Do not claim the app replaces official logbooks, certifies commercial driving hours, calculates legal compliance, provides navigation, tracks GPS, or replaces fleet management systems.

## Landing Copy

Record personal driving trips by vehicle, track distance from odometer readings, and review driving history by purpose, month, and vehicle.

## Video Prompt

Show a user adding a vehicle, recording a trip with start and end odometer readings, selecting a purpose, reviewing recent trips, and checking distance summaries by vehicle and purpose. Avoid GPS navigation visuals, official certification language, commercial driver compliance claims, fleet dashboards, or legal advice.

## Social Copy

Keep a personal driving history with vehicles, trips, purposes, distance, and notes in one calm logbook.

## SEO Notes

Focus on personal driver logbook, driving journal, trip log, mileage log, vehicle trip tracker, and personal driving history. Avoid official compliance, commercial driver certification, GPS tracking, and fleet-management claims.
