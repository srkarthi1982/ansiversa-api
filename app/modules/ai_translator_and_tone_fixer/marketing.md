## App Summary

AI Translator and Tone Fixer is an Ansiversa communication app that helps authenticated users organize translation projects, translation records, reusable templates, and history events. The current live V1 stores user-authored translation and tone-fix records with lightweight lists, detail endpoints, and owner-scoped access.

The product is designed for reviewable multilingual communication, not certified translation, legal or medical translation authority, real-time interpretation, localization management, or unmanaged AI execution.

## Brand Identity

AI Translator and Tone Fixer belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem. Every marketing asset should feel simple, trustworthy, intelligent, professional, accessible, and globally understandable.

The brand identity should communicate:

Avoid:

- Legal, medical, immigration, or official document authority.

## Core Value Proposition

Translation work often loses source context, tone intent, and review history. AI Translator and Tone Fixer gives users structured projects, translation records, reusable templates, and history so multilingual communication can be organized and revisited.

Its strongest value is reviewability. V1 stores workflow records and history but does not generate translations with an AI model. That boundary keeps marketing honest while preserving a strong foundation for future governed translation execution.

## Problems We Solve

- Source and translated text often become disconnected.
- Tone fixes can change meaning if users cannot review the context.
- Repeated phrases and response patterns need reusable templates.
- Translation history gets lost across notes, emails, and documents.
- Full text can be sensitive and should not appear casually in list screens.
- Users need clear boundaries around AI, high-stakes translation, and privacy.
- Multilingual communication needs organization, not just one-off output.
