# Car Pool Marketing

## Marketing Framework Version

Marketing Framework v1.0

## Current Product Truth

Car Pool is a Workflow Ready `comingSoon` Ansiversa mini app. It stores owner-scoped ride, passenger trip, and request records in an isolated backend database. It supports ride CRUD, duplicate, local join/leave, request approve/reject, and deterministic insights.

It does not provide live matching, payments, driver verification, public marketplace listings, chat, GPS tracking, emergency support, maps, notifications, or safety guarantees.

## Positioning

Car Pool helps users organize shared ride plans before real-world confirmation.

## Primary Message

Plan rides, manage local trip joins, review requests, and understand carpool activity in one protected workspace.

## Product Page CTA

Explore  
`/car-pool/rides`

## Audience

- Commuters planning repeated shared rides.
- Families and small groups coordinating pickups.
- Students and community members organizing one-time trips.
- Users who want structured carpool records without marketplace complexity.

## Feature Messaging

- Create ride offers with route, schedule, seats, meeting point, and status.
- Join or leave trips locally.
- Review pending, approved, and rejected requests.
- See seats offered, seats filled, completed trips, cancellation rate, and weekly activity.

## Guardrails

Do not claim that Car Pool verifies drivers, guarantees safety, processes payments, matches strangers, tracks vehicles, sends notifications, or replaces real-world confirmation.

## Short Copy

Car Pool organizes ride offers, local trips, passenger requests, and activity insights without turning carpool planning into a marketplace.

## SEO Notes

Use carpool planner, ride sharing organizer, shared ride tracker, passenger request tracker, and carpool coordination workspace.

## Video Prompt

Generate a premium realistic mobility productivity commercial for Ansiversa Car Pool. Show a user creating a shared ride, checking seats, joining a trip locally, reviewing requests, and opening a simple insights view. Keep the environment calm and everyday. Do not show live maps, payments, public rider matching, driver verification, emergency features, or GPS tracking.

## Social Copy

Organize shared rides without losing details in chat threads. Car Pool keeps rides, trips, requests, and activity in one protected Ansiversa workspace.
