## App Summary

Wellness and Goal Planner is an Ansiversa wellness app that helps authenticated users define wellness areas, set personal goals, and record dated reflections.

The current live V1 supports owner-scoped wellness areas, goals, and reflections. Goals include status, priority, progress, optional area, and optional target date. Reflections include date, mood, reflection body, optional energy level, notes, and optional links to areas or goals.

It does not send reminders, integrate calendars, provide medical guidance, generate wellness recommendations with AI, sync wearable data, calculate health scores, or act as a therapy, medical, or fitness-prescription product in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Its identity should feel gentle, reflective, non-clinical, organized, personal, and pressure-free.

Wellness and Goal Planner helps by connecting wellness categories, goals, progress, mood, notes, and reflection history.
