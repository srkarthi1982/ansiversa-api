## App Summary

Snippet Generator is an Ansiversa developer and productivity app that helps authenticated users create, organize, categorize, reuse, and manage reusable text or code snippets.

The current live V1 stores snippet projects, categories, editable snippets, and snippet history in an owner-scoped isolated backend. It supports organization and reuse, not AI-generated snippets.

It does not generate snippets with an AI model, provide syntax highlighting metadata, import/export snippets, search snippet bodies, favorite snippets, or support shared team libraries in the current version.

## Brand Identity

## Problems We Solve

Reusable snippets often get buried in old files, notes, chats, and bookmarks.

Snippet Generator helps by organizing snippets into owner-scoped projects, categories, editable records, and history events for creation, edit, copy, usage, versioning, and archive actions.
