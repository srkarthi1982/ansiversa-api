# Language Learning Buddy Marketing

## Positioning

**App:** Language Learning Buddy

**Category:** Personal Life & Wellness

**Status:** Approved Live v1.0

Language Learning Buddy helps Ansiversa users build a personal vocabulary list, record practice sessions, and review learning progress. It is positioned as a calm practice log, not a full language course or AI tutor.

## One-Line Value Proposition

Build vocabulary, record practice, and review progress in one focused language-learning workspace.

## Core Message

Language practice is easier to continue when words, attempts, and progress stay connected. Language Learning Buddy gives users a simple owner-scoped place to track what they are learning and how practice is going.

## Primary Audience

- Self-directed language learners.
- Students and professionals collecting practical vocabulary.
- Travelers and everyday learners who want a lightweight practice record.
- Ansiversa users who want language practice beside goals and study tools.

## Feature Highlights

- Protected Vocabulary workspace.
- Create, edit, update, and delete vocabulary records.
- Language, category, difficulty, and notes.
- Practice-session create, edit, update, and delete.
- Result and confidence tracking.
- Progress summaries and recent practice review.
- No browser storage persistence.
- No external AI in V1.

## Tone

Clear, practical, encouraging, and non-hype. Avoid fluency promises, miracle learning claims, and competitor-style lesson language.

## Product Boundaries

Language Learning Buddy does not provide generated lessons, AI conversation, pronunciation scoring, translation services, classroom management, public sharing, or certification.

## CTA

Primary CTA label: Explore

Primary CTA path: `/language-learning-buddy/vocabulary`

## Launch Copy

Language Learning Buddy helps you keep vocabulary and practice connected. Save words and translations, record practice sessions, and review your progress by language and difficulty.

## Short Variants

- Vocabulary and practice in one place.
- Build words. Record practice. Review progress.
- A simple language-learning log.
- Track the words you are learning.

## Visual Direction

Use a focused productivity workspace with vocabulary cards, practice records, and progress summaries. Avoid classroom stock imagery, flags as stereotypes, cartoon mascots, or AI-chat visuals for V1.

## Video Prompt Direction

Generate a realistic product-focused language-learning workspace scene for Language Learning Buddy by Ansiversa. Show a user saving vocabulary, recording a practice result, and reviewing progress summaries. The app should feel calm, focused, and practical. Avoid implying AI tutoring, guaranteed fluency, speech scoring, or full-course lessons.

## Compliance Notes

- Do not claim guaranteed fluency or retention.
- Do not imply external AI exists in V1.
- Do not copy competitor lesson designs, prompts, or language exercises.
- Keep messaging aligned with owner-scoped manual tracking.
