# Packing Checklist Marketing

## Positioning

Packing Checklist helps users prepare trips with organized lists, item categories, and clear packing progress.

## Audience

- Families preparing holidays, school trips, baby items, medicines, and documents.
- Professionals preparing work trips.
- Travelers, campers, sports travelers, and people relocating.

## Core Messages

- Build a packing list for every trip.
- Group items by category.
- Track packed, remaining, and high-priority items.
- Duplicate a previous checklist for the next journey.

## Feature Claims Allowed

- Checklist CRUD.
- Item CRUD.
- Category management.
- Duplicate/archive/restore/delete.
- Progress summaries from stored items.
- Search and filters.

## Feature Claims Not Allowed

- No guarantee that users will remember every required item.
- No compliance advice for visas, customs, airlines, medicine, or safety rules.
- No AI packing suggestions in V1.
- No collaboration or sharing claims.

## SEO Themes

packing checklist app, travel packing list, family packing planner, business trip packing checklist, camping packing list, moving checklist organizer.

## Short Copy

Plan the trip. Pack the items. See what remains.

## Video Prompt

Show a user creating a weekend trip checklist, adding documents, clothing, medicines, electronics, and camping items, then marking items packed as the progress meter rises. End with a calm screen showing remaining high-priority items.

## Guardrails

Marketing must describe the app as an organizer only. It must not imply official travel, medical, legal, customs, airline, or safety guidance.
