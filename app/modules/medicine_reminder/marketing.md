## App Summary

Medicine Reminder is an Ansiversa health-organization app that helps authenticated users create medicine records, define reminder schedules, log dose outcomes, and keep notes for refill or doctor conversations.

The current Workflow Ready V1 includes owner-scoped medicine CRUD, schedule CRUD, dose-log CRUD, note CRUD, dashboard summaries, filters, search, and an isolated backend database.

It does not include live notifications, clinical advice, dosage recommendations, drug-interaction checks, caregiver sharing, pharmacy integration, exports, or AI guidance in the current version.

## Core Value Proposition

Medicine Reminder gives users a structured place to manage medicine records, reminder schedules, dose logs, and notes so routines are easier to review and maintain.

The value is organization and memory support, not diagnosis, prescribing, or clinical decision-making.

## Problems We Solve

People often keep medicine instructions on bottles, schedules in memory, refill notes elsewhere, and dose history nowhere.

Medicine Reminder helps by:

- Keeping medicine details organized
- Defining schedule records
- Logging taken, missed, skipped, and late doses
- Showing active medicines and schedules
- Recording refill and doctor notes
- Keeping history owner-scoped and private

## Target Audience
