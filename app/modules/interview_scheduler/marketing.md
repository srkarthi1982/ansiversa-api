## App Summary

Interview Scheduler is an Ansiversa career app that helps signed-in users plan interview schedules, track interview rounds, and keep manual calendar events visible.

The current live V1 supports schedules, rounds, and calendar events. Schedules hold candidate and role context, rounds describe interview steps, and calendar events store timing and reminder details for planning visibility.

It does not send external calendar invites, send email reminders, integrate with calendar providers, or generate AI interview preparation in the current version.

Interview processes often involve multiple rounds, changing dates, and scattered notes.

Interview Scheduler helps by:

- Keeping interview schedules in one workspace

- Supporting owner-scoped scheduling records
