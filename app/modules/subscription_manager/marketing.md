# Subscription Manager Marketing

## Positioning

Subscription Manager is a private recurring-service tracker for people who want to understand what renews, when it renews, and how much it costs without connecting financial accounts or relying on provider integrations.

## Primary Message

Track subscriptions and renewal decisions before they surprise you.

## Value Propositions

- Keep subscription details in one private workspace.
- Group services by category.
- Record renewals manually.
- See upcoming, overdue, trial, and active subscriptions.
- Review currency-separated monthly and annual estimates.

## Boundaries

Subscription Manager V1 does not process payments, connect bank or card accounts, scan inboxes, ingest receipts, cancel third-party subscriptions, convert currencies, or verify charges.
