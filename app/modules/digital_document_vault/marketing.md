# Digital Document Vault Marketing

## Positioning

Digital Document Vault is a private Ansiversa workspace for uploading, organizing, and retrieving important personal document files.

## One-Liner

Upload important documents, organize them by category and type, and find them when you need them.

## Short Description

Digital Document Vault helps users keep personal document files organized with categories, tags, descriptions, optional dates, search, filters, and storage insights.

## Core Benefits

- Keep important files in a protected owner-scoped workspace.
- Organize documents with categories, types, tags, and descriptions.
- Track optional issue and expiry dates.
- Replace files without rebuilding metadata.
- Review total storage, category distribution, type distribution, expiry state, and recent uploads.

## Product Boundaries

Digital Document Vault is not a government portal, legal vault, identity wallet, cloud-drive client, official renewal service, or external verification product.

## Suggested Catalog Copy

A private digital document vault for uploading, organizing, searching, and reviewing personal files inside Ansiversa.
