# Doctor Visit Tracker Marketing

## Product Promise

Doctor Visit Tracker helps people keep doctor appointments, visit notes, follow-ups, and costs organized in one private workspace.

## Target Audiences

Individuals, parents, caregivers, and family organizers.

## Key Benefits

Track upcoming visits, completed history, specialties, follow-ups, costs, and insurance notes.

## Responsible Claims

Use planning and record-keeping language only. Do not claim diagnosis, treatment guidance, prescriptions, emergency response, or professional medical advice.

## CTA Ideas

Explore. Review visits. Track appointments.

## Video Direction

Show a user adding an upcoming appointment, recording a completed visit note, checking a follow-up date, and reviewing costs. Keep the message centered on personal organization.
