# Home Inventory Manager Marketing

## Positioning

Home Inventory Manager is a private Ansiversa workspace for organizing household belongings by category, room, condition, value, and warranty attention.

## One-Liner

Track household belongings, where they are kept, and which items need warranty review.

## Short Description

Home Inventory Manager helps users keep a lightweight record of personal belongings with categories, rooms, quantities, values, warranty dates, archive controls, and inventory insights.

## Core Benefits

- Keep household belongings organized in an owner-scoped workspace.
- Search and filter by category, room, condition, warranty state, and archive state.
- Record purchase dates, values, warranty dates, brand, model, serial number, and notes.
- Archive older items without losing the record.
- Review inventory totals, estimated value, distributions, recent additions, and high-value items.

## Product Boundaries

Home Inventory Manager is not ecommerce, ERP, warehouse management, marketplace inventory, accounting software, insurance software, barcode infrastructure, AI inventory, or smart-home integration.

## Suggested Catalog Copy

A private household inventory manager for tracking belongings, rooms, values, warranties, and archive status inside Ansiversa.
