# First Aid Guide Destination

## Destination Status

Unapproved for live launch. The app remains `active` / `comingSoon` / `version = null`.

## Product Destination

First Aid Guide should be a concise educational reference library for common first-aid awareness topics, with bookmarks and recent-view history.

## Principles

- Educational reference only.
- Encourage emergency services and professional care when appropriate.
- Avoid diagnosis, medication advice, and personalized treatment.
- Keep guide content concise and reviewed.
- Keep user-specific bookmarks/history owner scoped.

## V1 Scope

- System-managed categories and guides.
- Browse, search, filter, and sort guides.
- Guide detail view with steps, avoid actions, emergency warnings, prevention, and related topics.
- Bookmark and unbookmark guides.
- Recently viewed and most-viewed summaries.

## Non Goals

- Emergency dispatch.
- Replacing healthcare professionals.
- Diagnosis.
- Medication recommendations.
- AI-generated medical advice.
- External medical APIs.

## Journey Progress

V1 is prepared for manual verification and must remain behind the coming-soon gate until explicit approval.
