# First Aid Guide Marketing

## Positioning

First Aid Guide is an educational reference library for common first-aid awareness topics, bookmarks, and preparedness review.

## Guardrails

Do not claim the app replaces emergency services, doctors, healthcare professionals, diagnosis, medication guidance, or personalized treatment.

## Landing Copy

Browse concise first-aid reference topics, save favourites, and review emergency warning notes for personal preparedness.

## Video Prompt

Show a calm user browsing first-aid topics, filtering by category, opening a guide, reading concise steps and emergency warning notes, bookmarking a topic, and reviewing recently viewed topics. Avoid dramatic injury visuals, diagnosis claims, medication advice, or any suggestion that the app dispatches help.

## Social Copy

Keep first-aid reference topics organized for learning and preparedness. Contact local emergency services or qualified professionals when needed.

## SEO Notes

Focus on first aid guide, first aid reference, emergency preparedness, first aid topics, and educational first aid. Avoid treatment-promise language.
