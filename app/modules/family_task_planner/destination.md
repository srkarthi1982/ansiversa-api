# Family Task Planner Destination

## Product Destination

Family Task Planner should mature into a simple household coordination workspace for everyday responsibilities. Its destination is a calm family operations tool where users can see what must be done, who owns it, what is overdue, and what has recently been completed.

## Mature Workflow

The mature product should keep the core loop small:

* define family members
* define household categories
* create and assign tasks
* review today and upcoming work
* complete or reopen tasks
* understand workload and completion patterns

## Product Boundaries

Family Task Planner is not a project management suite, chat product, notification platform, or external calendar replacement. It should stay focused on household task coordination.
