# Family Task Planner Marketing

## Positioning

Family Task Planner is a household coordination app for keeping daily responsibilities visible, assigned, and completed.

## Audience

Families, couples, shared households, and caregivers who need a practical way to organize home responsibilities.

## Core Message

Plan family tasks, assign ownership, track due dates, and understand household workload without turning home life into a business project.

## Feature Highlights

* Household task dashboard
* Family member assignment
* Category organization
* Due date and recurring labels
* Complete, reopen, duplicate, edit, and delete actions
* Simple upcoming calendar
* Completion and workload insights

## Exclusions

No notifications, messaging, AI, payments, external calendar sync, email, SMS, or wearable integrations are included in the initial workflow.
