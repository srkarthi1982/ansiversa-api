# Meeting Scheduler Marketing Framework

## Positioning

Plan the meeting, keep the people and agenda together, and return to a clear scheduled record.

## Core message

Meeting Scheduler is a private Ansiversa workspace for organizing meeting details, participants, response labels, and discussion topics without scattered planning notes.

## Audience

- Individuals planning work or personal meetings
- Coordinators who need a simple participant checklist
- People who want an ordered agenda beside the schedule

## Supported claims

- Create, edit, search, filter, review, and delete meeting records.
- Track participants with pending, accepted, declined, or tentative labels.
- Keep ordered agenda items with durations and discussion status.
- Review upcoming, past, completed, cancelled, scheduled, and draft meetings.
- Store records in an authenticated owner-scoped workspace.

## Claims to avoid

Do not claim calendar synchronization, email invitations, automated reminders, live availability, conferencing, AI scheduling, or confirmed external responses. Meeting Scheduler records what the user enters; it does not contact participants.

## CTA

`Explore` opens `/meeting-scheduler/meetings`.
