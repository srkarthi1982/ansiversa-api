## App Summary

Job Tracker is an Ansiversa career app that helps signed-in users organize job listings, applications, and application insights during a job search.

The current live V1 supports job listings, applications, and insights. Application history exists in the backend for future workflow use but is not exposed in V1.

It does not scrape job boards, send reminders, parse resumes, generate AI recommendations, provide resume matching, or automate job applications in the current version.

## Core Value Proposition

Job Tracker gives users a structured workspace for manual job-search records.

The value is clarity across opportunities, applications, and insights, not automation or prediction.

## Problems We Solve

Job searches often spread across browser tabs, spreadsheets, email threads, and memory.

Job Tracker helps by:

- Saving job listings
- Tracking applications under jobs
- Recording application status
- Capturing insights and priorities
- Keeping follow-up context visible
- Separating active V1 features from future history workflows
