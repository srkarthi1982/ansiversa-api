# Household Expense Splitter Marketing

## Positioning

Household Expense Splitter is a private Ansiversa workspace for recording shared household expenses, member shares, settlements, and simple balances.

## One-Liner

Split shared household expenses and track who has paid whom back.

## Short Description

Household Expense Splitter helps roommates, couples, families, and shared living groups record expenses, split costs equally or manually, track settlements, and review simple household balances.

## Core Benefits

- Keep members and shared expenses in one owner-scoped workspace.
- Record equal and manual splits.
- Archive older expenses without losing the record.
- Track settlements made outside Ansiversa.
- Review simple member, category, balance, recent expense, and recent settlement insights.

## Product Boundaries

Household Expense Splitter is not banking, payment processing, accounting, invoicing, budgeting, OCR, receipt scanning, payroll, tax, ecommerce, subscriptions, or AI.

## Suggested Catalog Copy

A lightweight household expense splitter for recording shared costs, settlements, and simple member balances inside Ansiversa.
