## App Summary

Price Checker is an Ansiversa browser-local shopping utility for manually comparing product prices and organizing purchase decisions.

The current live V1 lets users create manual price analyses, save them locally, manage saved comparisons, and review local insights through Analyze, Analyses, and Insights routes. The backend only serves catalog and overview metadata.

It does not verify prices against live stores, normalize currencies, monitor price changes, send alerts, scrape pages, compare live listings, or sync data across devices in the current version.

## Problems We Solve

Purchase comparisons often happen across tabs, screenshots, notes, and memory.

Price Checker helps by:

- Recording product and store details
- Capturing category, current price, previous price, and notes
- Saving local analyses
- Supporting search, filtering, editing, duplication, and deletion
- Reviewing local insights
- Avoiding backend shopping history
