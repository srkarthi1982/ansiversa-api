# Decision Maker Marketing

## Positioning

Compare real options with criteria you define and scores you can inspect.

## Truthful capabilities

- Organize options, pros, cons, risks, assumptions, and notes.
- Normalize positive criterion weights transparently.
- Rate options on a 1–5 or 1–10 scale.
- Review complete-option rankings, ties, contributions, and a user-selected outcome.

Scores support structured thinking; they do not guarantee a correct decision or provide professional advice.
