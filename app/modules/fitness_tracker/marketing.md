## App Summary

Fitness Tracker is an Ansiversa wellness/productivity app that helps authenticated users create activities, log completed sessions, and review activity summaries.

The current V1 includes owner-scoped activity CRUD, log CRUD, dashboard summaries, activity filters, recent logs, and a protected Activities -> Logs -> Insights workflow.

It does not include medical advice, diagnosis, therapy, wearable sync, AI coaching, calorie estimates, workout prescription, or body-transformation promises.

## Core Value Proposition

Fitness Tracker gives users a focused place to record what they completed and review activity patterns over time. The value is personal visibility, not diagnosis, prescription, or guaranteed outcomes.

## Problems We Solve

People often lose track of completed activity when it is scattered across notes, memory, wearable apps, or social fitness feeds.

Fitness Tracker helps by:

- Keeping repeatable activities organized
- Recording completed sessions
- Capturing duration, intensity, effort, optional distance, and notes
- Showing recent logs and weekly totals
- Avoiding calories and health claims in V1

## Target Audience
