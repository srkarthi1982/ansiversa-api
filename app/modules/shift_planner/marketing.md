# Shift Planner Marketing

## Positioning
Plan clear shifts without turning a simple schedule into an HR system.

## Promise
Define reusable shift types, keep a lightweight member list, schedule daytime or overnight work, catch conflicts, and review net hours from one focused workspace.

## Feature language
- Reusable shift types and defaults
- Lightweight member assignments
- Daytime and overnight scheduling
- Break-aware net duration
- Search, filters, statuses, and metrics
- Conflict warnings for the same member

Shift Planner is a planning aid. It does not calculate pay, attendance, legal overtime, or labor compliance.
