## App Summary

Visiting Card Maker is an Ansiversa business and identity app that helps authenticated users create, edit, duplicate, list, and delete saved digital business card records.

The current live V1 stores owner-scoped card profile content and selected template data. Users can save professional contact information, select a template, edit cards, duplicate cards for variations, and delete cards.

It does not manage print orders, QR analytics, public hosted cards, binary exports, team brand kits, template authoring, shareable card URLs, or Portfolio Creator integration in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Visiting Card Maker belongs to the Ansiversa ecosystem.

Its identity should feel professional, personal, clean, practical, reusable, and contact-focused.
