## App Summary

Proposal Writer is an Ansiversa business writing app that helps authenticated users turn client opportunities into structured proposal projects, ordered sections, full drafts, and activity history.

The current live V1 supports manual proposal authoring and management. Users create projects, write sections such as scope and pricing, assemble draft proposals, and track review or submission activity.

It does not generate AI proposal content, export PDF or DOCX files, send proposals, provide approval workflows, or integrate with CRM or invoicing modules in the current version.

## Brand Identity

Its identity should feel professional, structured, client-aware, calm, trustworthy, and practical.
