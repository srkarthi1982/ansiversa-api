## App Summary

Formula Finder is an Ansiversa learning and reference app for quickly finding useful formulas from a bundled browser-local library.

The current live V1 supports local formula search, category filtering, copying, and browser-local favorites after users enter `/formula-finder/finder`. Backend responsibility is limited to catalog and overview metadata.

It does not store account-backed formula collections, user formulas, worked examples, analytics, symbolic derivation records, or server-side search in the current version.

It supports quick access and personal favorites without requiring server-side formula search or account-backed collections in V1.

## Problems We Solve

Users often lose time searching through notes, images, browser tabs, and scattered formula sheets.

Formula Finder helps by:

- Centralizing a compact formula reference
- Supporting local search
- Filtering by category
- Letting users copy formulas
- Saving favorite formula IDs locally
- Avoiding backend logging of formula search terms through app-specific endpoints
