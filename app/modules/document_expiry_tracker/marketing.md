# Document Expiry Tracker Marketing

## Positioning

Document Expiry Tracker is an Ansiversa documents and records app for people who need a private place to track passports, IDs, licences, permits, insurance records, certificates, and other personal document expiry dates.

## Current Product Promise

Keep important document dates visible, review what is expiring soon, and update records when renewal details change.

## Current Boundaries

V1 does not send notifications, emails, SMS, or push messages. It does not scan documents, upload files, extract data with OCR or AI, connect calendars, or integrate with government renewal systems.

## Primary Audience

- Travelers managing passports, visas, and residence permits.
- Households tracking IDs, licences, insurance, and certificates.
- Individuals who want a private renewal planning list inside Ansiversa.

## Main Workflow

Documents -> Expiry Dashboard -> Insights.

## Suggested Messaging

- "Track important document expiry dates in one private workspace."
- "See expired, expiring soon, and upcoming renewal windows."
- "Keep renewal planning organized without external integrations."

## Avoid

- Do not imply official renewal submission.
- Do not imply automated notification delivery.
- Do not imply OCR or document upload support.
- Do not imply secure document vault capabilities beyond metadata storage.
